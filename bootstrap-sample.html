What is Huyen's "usual" sushi order?

yellowtail

This is Huyen's favorite piece of fish. Soft and sweet but not quite oozing with fat. Tuna but not quite tuna. Basically delicious.

mackerel

Huyen used to not like mackerel, but she has since come to love its cured fishiness and lovely oily texture. Feels meaty.

uni

So buttery. So sweet. Great delicacy. My mouth waters just thinking about it.
